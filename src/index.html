For 2oo+ years we have been building homes the same way

With sticks, with hammers, and with billions of dollars a year spent in human resource costs.

We are going to build homes with robots.

The stud wall market alone is a one billion dollar a year market. Our first product, an automated stud wall manufactoring robot can completely build a 16' x 9' stud wall in ten minutes and reduce the size of work crews by 4x.

Since 2009 VC firms have gone from investing less than 25 million dollars a year to an expected 500 million dollars to robotics by the end of 2015.

A majority of that money has gone into the automotive industry, with less than 1% going into home construction. Advances in robot technology are allowing us to unlock that one billion dollar a year market.
